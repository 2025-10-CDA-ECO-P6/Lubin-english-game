Welcome to The English Game!

An immersive challenge to practise and improve your english level through an inter-school competition. For one week, you will work with your team to design a full serious game. Be prepared to get involved, create the best game and challenge yourself!

What's in store for you during this challenge?

  • An experience baseds on fun, learning and practice

    We hope that your week will be filled with learning and hands-on experience, but most of all that you will have lots of fun and enjoyment!

  • The basics of serious games through videos and quizzes

    A team of serious game experts will join you in the English Game to share their experience and teach you the fundamentals of game mechanics.

  • Tools to design a project from A to Z

    Each day will be based on a design process. From Monday to Friday, you will be supported as you design a project: define the concept, the level design, and the prototype, right up to producing the final game to present for the national competition.
